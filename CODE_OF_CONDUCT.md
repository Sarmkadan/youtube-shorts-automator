# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all people, regardless of background or identity. We pledge that the YouTube Shorts Automator project will be a harassment-free experience for everyone, and we are dedicated to providing an environment where all participants can contribute with respect and dignity.

## Expected Behavior

We expect all community members to:

- Be respectful and inclusive of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Give and gracefully accept constructive criticism
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members
- Be professional and courteous in all interactions
- Take responsibility and apologize to those affected if you say or do something harmful

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable within our community:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Discrimination based on race, gender, sexual orientation, disability, age, or other protected characteristics
- Intimidation or threats

## Reporting and Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by contacting:

- **Email**: rutova2@gmail.com
- **GitHub Issues**: [Report via Issues](https://github.com/sarmkadan/youtube-shorts-automator/issues)
- **Private Messages**: Contact a project maintainer directly

When reporting, please provide:
- A clear description of what happened
- Any relevant screenshots or logs
- Names of people involved (if applicable)
- How the behavior affected you

All reports will be treated confidentially.

### Enforcement

Project maintainers will:

1. **Acknowledge** receipt of the report within 48 hours
2. **Investigate** the complaint thoroughly and fairly
3. **Determine** the facts and severity of the violation
4. **Respond** appropriately with corrective action

Possible responses include:
- A private warning
- A public apology
- Temporary suspension from community spaces
- Permanent removal from the project

### Appeal Process

If you believe a decision was made unfairly, you may appeal by:

1. Emailing rutova2@gmail.com with "APPEAL:" in the subject line
2. Providing your reasoning for the appeal
3. A maintainer will review within 7 days

## Community Guidelines

### Be Respectful

- Listen to others without interrupting
- Acknowledge different perspectives
- Criticize ideas, not people
- Assume good intentions

### Be Inclusive

- Welcome newcomers and help them get started
- Use inclusive language (avoid gendered language, slurs, etc.)
- Be mindful of cultural differences
- Accommodate accessibility needs when possible

### Be Honest

- Give accurate information
- Admit mistakes and learn from them
- Give credit where it's due
- Be transparent about your limitations

### Be Collaborative

- Help others succeed
- Share knowledge and expertise
- Work together to solve problems
- Celebrate others' contributions

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- GitHub repository and discussions
- Project documentation and comments
- Pull request reviews and feedback
- Email communications about the project
- Community forums and chat platforms
- Project-related events

This Code of Conduct does not apply to:
- Personal matters unrelated to the project
- Discussions outside of project spaces
- Actions that don't impact the community

## Diversity and Inclusion

We believe that diversity strengthens our community. We actively encourage:

- People from all backgrounds to participate
- Different perspectives and approaches
- Constructive debate and disagreement
- Learning from each other's experiences
